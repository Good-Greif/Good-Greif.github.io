About Me/Skillz


As an experienced videographer, photographer, and avid artist, I have a broad range of skills and capabilities, including creating and capturing anything from advertisements, styling, portraits, weddings, music videos, to short films. With 6 years of experience in creating professional work, I have worked for ESPN+ and full-length feature films, including one starring Tom Green.

I am always seeking new knowledge and opportunities for growth. I was even awarded a scholarship to study Chinese in Taiwan, where I became HSK 3 conversational in Mandarin. In addition to my language skills, I am fluent in all of the the typical Adobe Suite programs, Final Cut, and DaVinci, and hold FAA 107 drone certification.

In pursuit of my passion, I earned a BS in Television and Film, completing my four-year degree in just three years. I am always learning new trends and keeping up with technology to deliver high-quality results. And not just creative skills, but I even managed to learn many technical skills.
