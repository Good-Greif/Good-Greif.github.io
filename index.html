Must Have Macarons N' More

Firstly, I started by researching the woman and her business. I wanted to understand her story, how she got started in baking, and what makes her business unique. I also wanted to get a sense of her personality and how she interacts with her customers. This research helped me plan out the video and determine what kind of shots I would need to capture.
Once I had a plan in place, I scheduled a day to film at her bakery. I arrived early to set up my equipment and make sure everything was ready to go. I then spent several hours filming her baking, interacting with customers, and sharing her story with me. I also took footage of the bakery itself, including shots of the delicious treats on display and the cozy atmosphere of the space.
After I finished filming, I went back to my editing suite and began piecing together the footage. I wanted to tell a story that captured the woman's love for baking, her journey to success, and the impact her business has on the local community. I carefully selected the best shots and edited them together to create a cohesive and engaging video. Overall, it was a fun and rewarding experience creating this video. I loved being able to showcase this woman's talent and passion, and I hope the video helps to promote her business and inspire others to pursue their dreams.
